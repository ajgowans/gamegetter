WIZZARD MATHS QUIZ

In this program, you will be given the chance to improve your
mathematical prowess. Wizzard will quiz you on any number of
questions and reward you with a funny face and an original
musical composition with every correct answer. By the way, if
you get the answer wrong, Wizzard can be quite rude! In fact,
he may simply choose not to accept your answer! You may choose
addition, subtraction, multiplication and division at any of
three levels of difficulty. We guarantee that this program will
challenge the entire family.
