COMPUTER STORY

With this program you will be able to 'write' your very own
story. All you have to do is provide details such as the name of
the male character, where he lives etc. You can have a great
deal of fun with this program! Wait until your friends see what
you have written about them! This program can even print a copy
of your masterpiece on paper for you (if you have a printer).
For all you budding authors, this is the program for you!.
